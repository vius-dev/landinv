Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 8, 2026

At Vius, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

We believe in data minimization. We only collect information that is necessary for the functionality of the app:

  • Account Information: When you sign up, we may collect your email address or phone number for authentication purposes.
  • Usage Data: We collect anonymous data about how you interact with the app to improve user experience (e.g., which politicians are viewed most often).
  • Device Information: We may collect basic device information (device type, OS version) for debugging and optimization.
  • User-Generated Content: Votes, ratings, and comments you post are public interactions within the app platform.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Vius service.
  • Authenticate your account and prevent fraud.
  • Analyze aggregate trends in political engagement (e.g., "Approval Ratings").
  • Send you important updates or notifications (which you can opt out of).

3. Data Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Your votes and ratings are anonymized in aggregate reports. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request the deletion of your account and data.
  • Opt-out of non-essential communications.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@viusapp.com.
